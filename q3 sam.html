Hunter x Hunter



SYNOPSIS

Hunter x hunter is a show about a kid that wants to be a hunter like his dad. He goes through the trials and tribulations of being a hunter like the hunter exam. He also meets friends along the way.

CHARACTERS
Gon Freecss
The main character of the series. He dreams to be a hunter to meet his long lost dad.
Killua Zoldyck
Killua was grown at a assasin household. He was tortured to grow up to be an assasin by his family. He is also in the main cast
Leorio Paladiknight
Aspires to be a doctor but doesn't have the money to do so. He does the hunter exam with gon so he can get a hunters license to finally be a doctor without much money.
